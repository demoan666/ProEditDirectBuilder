How to Use ProEdit Direct Builder

Workflow

  1. Click Choose Video to automatically import and link a video file.
  2. Load your script — Choose File (.docx, .xlsx, .csv, .txt).
  3. Use the Checkboxes to choose your passes (Build Sequence vs Overlays Only).
  4. Click Build Timeline.

Supported Formats & PDFs

  • Best for Columns: Excel (.xlsx), CSV
  • Best for Standard Text: Word (.docx), Text (.txt)
  • PDF Workaround: PDFs are not supported natively. Easily bypass this by copying the text from your PDF and pasting it directly into the text box.

Caption Styling

After dragging your generated .srt onto the timeline, highlight the blocks, format the font in the Essential Graphics panel, and save it as a "Track Style" to style them all instantly.
